Pears History

Pears are fruits produced and consumed around the world, growing on a tree and harvested in late summer into mid-autumn.
The pear tree and shrub are a species of genus Pyrus in the family Rosaceae, bearing the pomaceous fruit of the same name.
Several species of pears are valued for their edible fruit and juices, while others are cultivated as trees.
