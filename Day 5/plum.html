Plum History

Plums belong to the same family as peaches, nectarines, and apricots.
But plums are much more diverse than their stone-fruit cousins.
They can be large or small, with red, purple, green, yellow, or orange skin, and pink, yellow, or orange flesh.
They first grew in China thousands of years ago.
